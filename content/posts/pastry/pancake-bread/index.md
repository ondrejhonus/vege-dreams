This is a high protein pancake bread, that fills you up for a long time, and feels very satisfying, you can also make it into a portable glass container (oven-safe) and take it to work. This should be an okay portion for a sweet lunch (but of course you're always gonna want more, because of how good it is).

<div class="recipe" id="recipe">
Prep time: 8 minutes

Cooking time: 25 minutes

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 Egg
- [ ] 60g Plain greek yogurt
- [ ] 20g Chicory syrup
- [ ] 40g All-purpose flour
- [ ] 1/2 tsp Baking powder 
- [ ] Frozen berries (or fresh if you have some)
<hr>

### Steps
1. **Preheat oven**
    Preheat your oven to 180ËšC.

2. **Whisk egg:**  
   In a medium bowl, whisk the egg until it's a bit frothy/pale

3. **Add in the rest of the wet ingredients:**  
   Add the yogurt and chicory syrup and combine.

4. **Add the dry:**  
   Fold in flour and baking powder. Mix just until the flour dissapears, dont overmix or you'll end up with a brick.

5. **Berries**
    Pour the batter into a **greased** oven-safe container and drop your berries on top.

6. **Bake**
    Bake for 20-35 minutes, or until the center is firm and a toothpick comes out without raw batter (might have berry juice on it).
